// PEST CONTROL — MECHANICAL & CHEMICAL SOLUTIONS

⚠ Rats — Attack Vector
Rats climb cage stands and steal larvae. Traditional wire netting is insufficient — rats chew through or squeeze past.
✓ Grease Barrier / Water Moat — Solution
Heavy grease or old motor oil applied to cage stand legs — rat paws get no grip on slippery surface, cannot climb. Alternatively: each cage leg sits inside a small water bowl. Rats refuse to wade through water. Island effect: BSF bottle surrounded by water basin.
⚠ Birds — Larva Theft
Birds dive for exposed BSF larvae from the collection bottle or open bins. Direct-landing theft occurs rapidly.
✓ Island Effect — Solution
Collection bottle surrounded by water basin at all sides. Birds cannot land directly on water surface — island effect prevents all access. 1.5cm bottle entry hole also bird-beak proof.
⚠ Cockroaches — Colony Infestation
Cockroach colonies breed rapidly in warm cage environments, contaminating feed and threatening flock health.
✓ Fipronil Gel + Boric Acid — Ninja Wipeout
Commercial Fipronil Gel Bait (syringe-applied in dots under cages). DIY alternative: 1 tsp boric acid powder + 1 tsp condensed milk/sugar + flour + water → shaped into small balls → placed under cage. Roaches eat, die inside colony — nest-mates cannibalize corpse → chain reaction wipes entire colony.
